Development of a SaaS Product in the field of Healthcare

Link for Software Prototype

Ergonomic Evaluation of Human-Product Interface in Laparoscopic Surgery


• Under the funding of Government Of India ( In collaboration with PURDUE University, Indiana, US ) this SPARC project has the application of ergonomics.

• Analysed 110+ research papers and conducted pilot survey for deciding the department of healthcare.

• Design aspects such as postural analysis, anthropometric match, physical and mental work capacity are analysed using 3dSSPP software.

Application of Simulation for tracking the flow of the faculty at different levels in IIT System

• Project includes the analysis of effects if the mandatory retirement age barrier for the faculty is removed.

• ARENA SIMULATOR software is utilised for the analysis.

• Create, Process, Assign, Decision, Record and Dispose Modules are used in simulation of the model.

Designing And Optimizing The Security Inspection System At The AirPort

• Under the application of work system design (also through virtual reality), designed the work frame of airport security system to optimize it ergonomically.

• Performed time study to trace the bottleneck in the existing workframe of airport security system.

• Process Diagram and User Case Diagram of Security Inspection System of Airport were also redesigned for study and analysis of bottleneck in the work system.

Genetic Algorithm based model for Optimizing Bank Lending Decisions


• Applied genetic algorithm for optimizing Bank lending decision under various constraints.

• Used amalgamation of algorithms (Genetic algorithm + Simulated annealing) to get the best fitness value of 4.15.

• Decision variables are loan age, loan size, credit limit, loan type, loan interest rate and expected loan loss.
